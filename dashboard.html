Behavioral Signals Monitored
😴 Sleep Hours
Normal: 7-9h. Under 5h or over 10h signals risk.
🚶 Activity Steps
Normal: 5,000-10,000. Under 2,000 indicates sedentary depression risk.
📱 Screen Time
Over 8h/day correlates with anxiety and mood disorders.
🤝 Social Interactions
Under 2/day signals withdrawal — key depression indicator.
💓 HRV (ms)
Low HRV under 20ms = autonomic stress and anxiety.
📲 App Usage Variance
High variance over 0.6 may indicate manic episodes.
⌨️ Typing Speed Change
Slowing below -0.15 correlates with psychomotor retardation.
🗺️ Location Entropy
Under 0.3 indicates social isolation and restricted mobility.
